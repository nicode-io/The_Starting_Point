Sommelier

Making sure you always choose the wine that will enhance your tasting is an exciting job every day. I travel the world, with you, through the vineyards.

Founded in the 90s, the Pigeniro allowed a mix of cultures and a discovery of the world through its plates. It reinvents itself every day with a team led by our renowned chef, Anne Aston. Sharing a moment of conviviality at one of our tables will always be an opportunity to discover new dishes and remarkable products. Our desire is always the same, to offer everyone the chance to enjoy exceptional cuisine in all simplicity.

At Pigeniro, all our products are rigorously selected to guarantee an exceptional origin and to favour local producers from our regions as much as possible. We work hand in hand with the producers in order to ensure an explosion of tastes during your tastings. A good product always starts with a lot of passion.

Brussels, capital of Europe and centre of Belgium, as close to the sea as to the Ardennes forest, it gathers in it all this mixture of culture which makes its enormous richness. An essential step in your discovery of Europe. Visit also Bruges and its exceptional preserved historical architecture. Many other treasures are also to be discovered, welcome to Belgium !
